About Book


According to world health organization, Cancer is the second foremost reason of death worldwide. Only in 2018, about 9.6 million people have died due to cancer globally. The most common cancers are: Lung, Breast, Colorectal, Prostate, Skin cancer, and Stomach. The scientists, researchers and oncologists agree on the fact that cancer mortality can be reduced if cases are detected and treated early. When recognized prematurely, cancer is more likely to respond to effective treatment and can result in a greater probability of surviving, less morbidity, and less expensive treatment. Unfortunately, most of the cancer patients are identified in the last stages of the syndrome. In advanced points, the tumour has spread excessively leaving no better treatment choices for either stopping the metastasis or complete removal of cancer. In recent times, artificial intelligence based computer aided diagnoses system have shown major significance in solving this issue. The application of artificial intelligence for the betterment of healthcare is booming with each passing day. The book will drop radiance on the reasons for the growing profusion and complexity of data in this sector. Further, the book will help to focus on further research challenges and directions for the practitioners as well as researchers. Also, a special emphasis will be focused on collections of useful advance mechanisms, automated tools and possible directions for solving complex problem. The book will contain volumes of information about advance machine learning approaches spanning the areas of neural networks, fuzzy logic, connectionist systems, genetic algorithms, evolutionary computation, cellular automata, self-organizing systems, fuzzy systems, and hybrid intelligent systems in solving the cancer diseases.


Topics Covered


This book is targeted towards scientists, application doctors, health professionals, professors and students working in cancer research. This book will reveal different dimensions of Machine Learning applications and will illustrate its use in the solution of assorted real world healthcare problems. Moreover, the proposed book will also cover distinctive features and techniques those are listed below and will magnetize prospective audience:


Topics of interest include, but are not limited to, the following:


  • Data Analysis in Cancer disease

  • Learning from multiple modalities of imaging data for cancer detection/diagnosis

  • Neural Network for Lung cancer diagnosis

  • Probabilistic Methods for thyroid cancer diagnosis

  • Neuro fuzzy based technique for Oral Cancer diagnosis

  • Neuro-Evolutionary approach/Intelligent Technique for Colon cancer diagnosis

  • Intelligent Approach for Multiple myeloma diagnosis

  • ANN and Swarm based approach for Skin cancer diagnosis

  • Deep Learning in Brest Cancer diagnosis

  • Automated cervical cancer diagnosis

  • Convolutional Neural Network in head and neck cancer prognosis

  • Issues and Future Challenges in Cancer prognosis : (Prostate cancer : case study)

  • All other Cancer research

  • Applications of advance ML methods in Cancer classification

    • We strongly welcome other topic suggestions (Any Cancer research), dealing with the applications on State-of-the-Art methods of Machine Learning related areas.

    Students, Academic scientists, researchers and health practitioners are invited to submit a brief abstract (within 250 words) along with author details and affiliations of their full chapters for this edited book entitled “Advance Machine Learning Approaches in Cancer Prognosis: Challenges and Applications” on or before 10th MAY, 2020. After finalization of abstracts, they may submit their full papers by 10th AUGUST 2020. All submissions must be original and should not be under review by another publication. Authors should make sure that their submission has 15% or lower similarity as per iThenticate or Turnitin. All submitted chapter will be reviewed on a double-blind, peer review basis. The book chapters should contain no less than 20 pages/no more than 30 pages of content as per the provided format.


    ***There is no submission fees for this publication. It’s completely free of cost.
